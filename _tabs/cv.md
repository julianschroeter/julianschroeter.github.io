# CV

- **Since April 2023:** Professor of Digital Literary Studies, LMU Munich
- **July 2025:** Habilitation, *Venia legendi* in Modern German Literature and Digital Humanities, University of Würzburg
- **August 2022–February 2023:** Walter Benjamin Fellow, School of Information Sciences, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
- **March–July 2022:** Walter Benjamin Fellow, University of Antwerp
- **April 2021–March 2023:** Postdoctoral researcher, Digital Humanities/German Literature, University of Würzburg
- **April–September 2020:** Deputy Professor of Digital Humanities, University of Trier
- **July 2020:** Admission to the habilitation process in Digital Humanities/German Literature, University of Würzburg. Topic: a digital history of novellas and journal prose in the nineteenth century
- **October 2011–March 2021:** Research Associate, Digital Humanities/German Literature, University of Würzburg
- **2017:** PhD, University of Würzburg. Dissertation: *Theorie der literarischen Selbstdarstellung*. Awarded the Promotionspreis der Universität Würzburg and the Unterfränkische Gedenkjahrstiftung prize in 2018
- **2006–2011:** Magister Artium in German Literature and Philosophy, University of Würzburg
- **2005–2006:** Civilian service
- **2005:** Abitur
